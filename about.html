About

Dr Carton trained as an undergraduate at Christ’s College, Cambridge where he achieved a triple first class degree with distinction in pathology. His postgraduate specialist training in cellular pathology was undertaken in Oxford and he became a Fellow of the Royal College of Pathologists in 2008.

Dr Carton has been a consultant histopathologist at Imperial College Healthcare NHS Trust since 2009 where he specialises in dermatopathology and uropathology. He teaches medical students and histopathology trainees and is also the author of the Oxford Handbook of Clinical Pathology, part of the world renowned Oxford Medical Handbook series.

Dr Carton participates in the national dermatopathology and uropathology EQA schemes.
