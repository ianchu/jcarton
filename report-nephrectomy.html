Example nephrectomy report

CLINICAL DETAILS:
Left radical nephrectomy for renal mass.

MACROSCOPIC:
Pot labelled “left kidney”: A radical nephrectomy specimen weighing 392 g. The kidney measures 17 x 10 x 8 cm with surrounding perinephric fat. Adrenal gland is also included measuring 5 x 2 x 2 cm. On slicing the kidney a solid tumour is present in the upper pole with haemorrhagic and golden yellow areas. The tumour measures up to 5.2 cm in size. Tumour appears confined to the kidney macroscopically with no apparent invasion of perinephric fat, renal sinus or the main renal vein. Blocks: A1: vascular and ureteric margins, A2: background kidney, A3: adrenal, A4-10: tumour.

MICROSCOPIC:
Tumour type: Clear cell renal cell carcinoma
ISUP grade: 3
Necrosis: Yes (~10% of tumour)
Sarcomatoid change: No
Invasion of perirenal fat: No
Invasion of renal sinus: No
Main renal vein invasion: No
Adrenal involvement: No
Margin status: Clear
TNM 7 tumour stage: pT1b
Leibovich score: 4 (intermediate risk)

DIAGNOSIS:
LEFT RADICAL NEPHRECTOMY: 5.2 CM CLEAR CELL RENAL CELL CARCINOMA, GRADE 3, STAGE pT1b, EXCISED.
