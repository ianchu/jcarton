Dermatopathology

Dr Carton has specialised in dermatopathology since becoming a consultant and has attended the advanced dermatopathology course run by Dr Eduardo Calonje at the St John’s Institute of Dermatology.

He is one of four consultant histopathologists providing specialist dermatopathology services for Imperial College Healthcare NHS Trust and Chelsea & Westminster NHS Trust.

He reports the full range of inflammatory and neoplastic dermatopathology with particular subspecialist expertise in alopecia and genital dermatopathology. Dr Carton works particularly closely with the dermatology and GUM departments at St Mary’s Hospital including attending regular cancer and clinicopathological meetings. All skin cancer reports are completed in line with current RCPath guidelines. The importance of CPC discussion of complex cases is recognised and encouraged.
