Example prostatectomy report

CLINICAL DETAILS:
Lap radical prostatectomy for GS 3+4 Ca prostate.

MACROSCOPIC:
Pot labelled “prostate”: A radical prostatectomy specimen weighing 60 g. The prostate measures 5 x 4.5 x 4 cm with attached bilateral seminal vesicles. Margins are inked as follows: left = blue, right = green, anterior = yellow. Blocks: A1-4: apex, A5-9: base, A10-11: seminal vesicles, A12-18: megablocks of prostate from apex to base.

MICROSCOPIC:

TUMOUR
Acinar type adenocarcinoma, Gleason score 3 + 4 = 7 is present in the left and right peripheral zones. The left sided nodule has a tumour volume of 1.4 ml and the right sided nodule has a tumour volume of 0.5 ml. Total tumour volume is 1.9 ml.

SPREAD
Perineural invasion: Yes
Lymphovascular invasion: No
Extraprostatic extension: No
Seminal vesicle involvement: No
Bladder neck involvement: No

MARGINS
Apex margin: Clear (0.5 mm)
Circumferential margin: Clear (1 mm)
Base margin: Widely clear

TNM 7 STAGE
pT2c

DIAGNOSIS:
RADICAL PROSTATECTOMY: ADENOCARCINOMA, GLEASON SCORE 3 + 4 = 7, STAGE pT2c, EXCISED.
